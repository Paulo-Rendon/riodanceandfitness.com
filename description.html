Zumba

Zumba is a fun, high energy workout that moves between high and low intensity dance moves set to Latin and International music, designed to get your heart rate up, blast calories, and boost endurance. Have fun moving to the music regardless of your dance and fitness levels. Zumba is a trademark owned by Zumba Fitness, LLC.

HIIT

We have cardio, body weight, and dumbbell HIIT workouts. High-intensity interval training (HIIT) is a training protocol alternating short periods of intense or explosive anaerobic exercise with brief recovery periods until the point of exhaustion. HIIT involves exercises performed in repeated quick bursts at maximum or near maximal effort with periods of rest. HIIT is a fantastic way to lose weight, and tone up quickly.

Dance Fitness

Dance Fitness is a Sassy-styled workout rooted in traditional Hip-Hop, we combine cardio and toning for a well rounded, full body workout for all levels. Dance has far reaching physical, and psychological benefits. It can benefit both physical and mental health. This dance class has easy to follow choreography used to create dance workout routines that are easy enough to follow, yet challenging and effective.
